# Business Case: QuickFixU

## Executive Summary

QuickFixU is a marketplace that connects clients with trusted service professionals such as electricians, plumbers, and gas technicians. The platform solves two connected problems: clients need a reliable way to find and compare professionals, and professionals need a better digital channel to receive qualified work opportunities.

The V1 business direction is intentionally simple: QuickFixU coordinates discovery, communication, proposals, scheduling, and completion confirmation, while payment happens outside the app through cash or bank transfer. The platform does not process money, store payment methods, or act as a financial intermediary in V1.

## Problem

### For clients
- It is hard to quickly find reliable professionals for home service problems.
- Comparing options, availability, and reputation is fragmented and informal.
- Coordination often happens through unstructured calls or chat without clear records.

### For professionals
- Independent professionals depend on word of mouth or expensive acquisition channels.
- Work demand is irregular and difficult to predict.
- Many do quality work but lack digital visibility, social proof, and structured opportunities.

## Solution

QuickFixU offers a simple service marketplace with two entry points for clients:

1. **Search from a list of professionals** using category, location, and reputation.
2. **Publish a problem/request** and receive proposals from professionals.

The platform keeps the workflow inside QuickFixU for:
- discovery,
- profile review,
- proposals,
- chat and coordination,
- visit/work confirmation,
- completion confirmation by both sides,
- mutual reviews.

**Payment remains external** to the platform in V1.

## V1 Value Proposition

### For clients
- Faster access to verified professionals.
- Clearer comparison of proposals and profiles.
- Structured coordination and traceability inside the app.
- Completion confirmation and reviews that improve trust.

### For professionals
- More visibility in their service area.
- Direct access to client requests/problems.
- Better conversion through proposals and in-app coordination.
- Reputation building through reviews and verified profiles.

## Business Model

### V1
V1 does **not** monetize through in-app transaction commissions.

The first release is focused on validating supply, demand, workflow adoption, and trust signals without adding payment-processing complexity, regulatory exposure, or financial operations.

### Future monetization
The primary monetization path after V1 is **subscription-based**, especially for professionals. Examples:
- verified badge,
- ranking boost,
- higher visibility in listings,
- better recommendation placement,
- premium profile presentation.

This keeps the business aligned with marketplace value creation instead of payment intermediation.

**Boundary note:** this is a post-V1 commercial direction only. It does not authorize adding subscription schema, billing endpoints, invoices, entitlements, paywalls, or mobile purchase UX to the current release.

**Trust rule:** V1 verified/certified signals remain trust features, not paid unlocks.

## V1 Scope Boundaries

### Included in V1
- Client and professional onboarding.
- Professional profiles with specialties, availability, and verification status.
- Search and filtering of professionals.
- Publishing requests/problems.
- Receiving and managing proposals.
- In-app chat and coordination.
- Work status tracking.
- Completion confirmation by client and professional.
- Ratings and reviews.

### Explicitly out of scope for V1
- Credit card payments.
- Payment gateways.
- Escrow or retained funds.
- In-app transaction commissions.
- Wallets or balances.
- Storing payment methods.
- Refund flows handled by the platform.

## Market Opportunity

QuickFixU starts with essential home-service categories in Argentina, where trust, speed, and reputation matter more than sophisticated fintech flows for early adoption. By reducing operational scope in V1, the product can validate the marketplace loop faster and with less implementation risk.

## Risks and Mitigation

### Risk: users move final payment outside the platform
This is acceptable in V1 by design. The platform competes on discovery, coordination, trust, and service quality tracking rather than payment control.

### Risk: lower short-term monetization
Mitigation: prioritize supply quality, reviews, and professional visibility features that can later support subscription upsells.

### Risk: trust without financial guarantees
Mitigation: reinforce verified professionals, proposal records, in-app coordination, mutual completion confirmation, and reviews.

## Strategic Rationale for the Pivot

Removing in-app payments from V1 reduces:
- product complexity,
- compliance and financial risk,
- support burden around disputes and money handling,
- time to launch the first usable marketplace.

This lets QuickFixU focus on the CORE loop: connect clients with professionals, help them coordinate effectively, and confirm completed work.
